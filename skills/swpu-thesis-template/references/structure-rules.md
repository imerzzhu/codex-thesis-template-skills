# SWPU Thesis Structure Rules

These rules are extracted from the supplied SWPU Electrical Engineering and Information undergraduate thesis template.

## Required Order

1. Chinese cover
2. English cover
3. Chinese abstract
4. English abstract
5. Contents
6.正文 chapters
7. `7 结论与展望`
8. `谢 辞`
9. `参考文献`
10. `附 录`

## Cover Pages

- Chinese cover includes: `本科毕业设计（论文）`, title, student name, student number, school/college, major and grade, first supervisor, supervisor title/unit, assistant supervisor, assistant title/unit, and completion date.
- English cover includes: `Southwest Petroleum University`, `Graduation Thesis`, English title, grade, name, speciality, instructor, school name, and date.
- The supplied template uses `School of Electrical Engineering and Information` on the English cover.

## Abstracts

- Chinese abstract starts with `摘  要`.
- Chinese abstract should summarize content, method, result, and conclusion; do not use formulas, charts, or citation numbers in the abstract.
- Chinese abstract length is generally about 500 Chinese characters.
- Suggested Chinese abstract structure: background/current situation, what was done and how, and resulting functions/data/value.
- English abstract starts with `Abstract`.
- Chinese and English abstracts have no header or footer.
- Keywords use `关键词：关键词1；关键词2；关键词3` and `Keywords: Keywords1; Keywords2; Keywords3`.

## Contents

- Contents heading is `目  录`.
- Contents page has no header and may use Roman-numeral footer/page numbering.
- The template TOC includes level 1, level 2, and level 3 entries, plus `谢 辞`, `参考文献`, and `附 录`.
- Regenerate or update Word TOC fields after heading edits.

## Body Chapters

-正文 begins with `1 绪论`.
- The template uses chapters 1 through 7:
  - `1 绪论`
  - `2 第2章标题`
  - `3 第3章标题`
  - `4 第4章标题`
  - `5 第5章标题`
  - `6 第6章标题`
  - `7 结论与展望`
- Each main chapter starts on a new page.
- `绪论` should introduce background/purpose, domestic and foreign research, design/research methods, process, and content.
- `结论与展望` should state achieved functions/results and should not be only a generic summary. Outlook should be limited.
- Use one consistent self-reference throughout, such as `本设计`, `本文`, or `本系统`, rather than switching among them.

## Headers And Footers

- Chinese and English abstracts have no header/footer.
- Contents has no header and may use Roman page numbering in the footer.
-正文 odd pages: header is the thesis title.
-正文 even pages: header is `西南石油大学本科毕业设计（论文）`.
-正文 page numbers appear in the footer.

## Acknowledgements

- Heading: `谢 辞`.
- Acknowledgements should briefly thank people who directly contributed to research and thesis writing, such as supervisors, assistant supervisors, and others who helped.

## Appendix

- Heading: `附 录`.
- If there is only one appendix, remove appendix subcategory labels such as `附录一：`.
- If appendix content is program code, place the code in a separate Word document named `姓名-学号-论文附件` and state in the appendix that the code is in the thesis attachment.
